Some rules you need to remember about ABS workout


1. Never do crunches

2. Don’t work your abs every day

3. Planks are king

4. Add extra resistance

5. Do your reps quickly for the most burn

6. Do your ab training first, and then cardio after

7. Breathe through the exercises

Some rules you need to remember about Legs workout


1. Attack Legs As A Weak Point

2. Start With Squats. Period.

3. Go Deep

4. Change Up Your Foot Position

5. Use High And Low Foot Placements

6. Focus On Your Quads

8. Split Your Leg Workout
